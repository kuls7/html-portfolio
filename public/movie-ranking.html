The best movies if you want a good cry

My top 3 movies of all time

Taare Zameen Par

It's a beautiful story of a dyslexic child finding true support and uncovering his potential. It's a tear- jerker because it shows the terrible indian standards for academic performance and the pain both parents and their child go through to survive.

The Pursuit of Happyness

A father and his young son are evicted from his apartment and live in a shelter, facing hardships, making sacrifices and srviving to make ends meet. I bawled my eyes out throughout the movie.

Masaan

a heartbreaking love-story of a young man falling in love with a young girl from a different caste. The moral traditions, cruel punishments for not adhering to the traditions, the pain and sacrifice of people sincerely in love but not being able to do anything to acheive it- takes you on a rollercoaster of emotions, mainly giref.
